Carmel Hazelnut Mocha

Servings 2
Energy 380 calories

Ingredients:

  • Caramel Pretzel Stirrers (optional, see below)
  • 3/4 cup chocolate-hazelnut spread
  • 1/2 cup caramel dessert topping or dulce de leche sauce
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee powder
  • 8 cups milk (fat-free, 2% or whole)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Method:

  • In Dutch oven or large saucepan place chocolate-hazelnut spread, caramel dessert topping, cocoa powder and coffee powder. Stir in milk. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until hot. Do not boil. Remove from heat. Stir in vanilla. Ladle milk mixture into ten 8- to 10-ounce mugs. Serve with Caramel Pretzel Stirrers, if desired.
