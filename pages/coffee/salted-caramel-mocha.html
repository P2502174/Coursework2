Salted Caramel Mocha

Servings 1
Energy 100.9 calories

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup brewed coffee cooled
  • ¼ cup unsweetened vanilla almond milk or other milk of your choice1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 1 Tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ teaspoon caramel extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt1
  • ½ - 2 cups ice Optional: whipped topping and/or salted caramel syrup for garnish

Method:

  • Add all ingredients to blender and combine! Garnish with whipped topping and salted caramel syrup, if desired.
