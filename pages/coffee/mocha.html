Mocha

Servings 2
Energy 187 calories

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons Instant coffee
  • 2 tablespoons Sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Hot water
  • Tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) Milk of your choice, cold or hot
  • 2-3 Ice cubes if you are making cold coffee

Method:

  • In a large mixing bowl place coffee, sugar and hot water (1:1:1 ratio). Using a hand mixer or electric mixer, whisk until thick and creamy. When its thick and creamy add cocoa powder and keep whisking until combined and thick, and creamy. Fill only 3/4 of a cup with cold milk and add ice cubes. Top with foamy whipped coffee, grate some dark chocolate. Mix well before drink. Enjoy!
