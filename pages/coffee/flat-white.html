Flat White

Servings 1
Energy 1.95 calories

Ingredients:

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 2 shots foamed milk

Method:

  • Gather three ingredients. Pull 1 shot espresso. While pulling the shot, froth the milk. As the milk froths, use a spoon to fold the microbubbles from the top of the steaming pups with lemon twists if desired. Gather thither to the bottom of the steaming pitcher. This will create a smoother, more velvety texture for your drink. Combine the two ingredients. (Latte art optional). Your flat white should have about 1/4-inch of steamed milk on top.
