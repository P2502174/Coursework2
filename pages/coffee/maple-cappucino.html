Maple Cappucino

Servings 1
Energy 175 calories

Ingredients:

  • 2 teaspoons maple syrup
  • 1⁄2cup espresso coffee, freshly prepared
  • 3⁄4cup milk
  • cocoa powder, to dust

Method:

  • Put maple syrup in the bottom of a mug. Foam & heat the milk. Add the milk to the mug and then the coffee. If you want the layered look as per the photo, pour the milk & coffee over the back of a spoon when adding to the mug. Top with milk froth & dust with cocoa powder.
